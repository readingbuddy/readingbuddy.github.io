About

Want to take a break from hanging out with your little friend? Let Reading Bubby help you get a few minutes of break! We bring you one story every week. Better yet, we teach our little friends one conversational phrase in Chinese with every story. Presently we source stories from the public domain. If you'd like to grant permission for us to read copyrighted stories, please reach out. Don't forget to check out the Coloring Pages session for some fun time together.
